Anagram is the most powerful bass-specific multi-effect processor available. Cognate Audio is proud to join the Anagram ecosystem with a suite of blocks designed for the modern bassist.

We've used our 30 years of DSP and audio software experience, but we haven't rehashed any existing plugins for this collection — all our work is crafted bespoke to harness the power of Anagram to give you musically intelligent and useful bass blocks.
