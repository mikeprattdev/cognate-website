About Cognate Audio

Precision audio tools for bass players, designed by bassists for bassists.

Cognate Audio was born from a simple idea: bass players deserve tools that understand their unique needs. As working musicians, we know the frustration of gear that almost works, effects that track too slowly, and practice tools that feel like an afterthought.

When Darkglass released the Anagram, we saw an opportunity. Here was a platform powerful enough to run real DSP algorithms, portable enough to fit on any pedalboard, and open enough to let us build exactly what we wanted.

Every Cognate block is designed with bass in mind from the ground up. Our pitch tracking algorithms are optimised for the low frequencies and complex harmonics of bass guitar. Our practice tools work the way bass players actually practice. Our effects are voiced to sit perfectly in the mix.

Built for Anagram

The Perfect Platform

Darkglass Anagram is a revolutionary audio processor that combines powerful DSP with the practicality of a stompbox. Its open architecture lets developers like us create custom effects and tools that run directly on the hardware.

All Cognate blocks are available exclusively through the Anagram Store, where they integrate seamlessly with your existing signal chain.
