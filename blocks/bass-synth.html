About Cognate Bass Synth

Cognate Bass Synth brings the world of analog synthesizers to your pedalboard. Using advanced pitch tracking technology, it converts your bass playing into control signals that drive a fully-featured synthesizer engine.

Unlike traditional octave effects or simple pitch shifters, Cognate Bass Synth generates entirely new sounds from scratch. The result is a rich, full synth tone that responds to your playing dynamics and articulation.

The built-in filter section with envelope control lets you create classic synth bass sounds with ease. From deep, rumbling subs to cutting lead lines, Cognate Bass Synth gives you the tools to expand your sonic palette.
